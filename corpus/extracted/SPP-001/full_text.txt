STATE PLANNING FRAMEWORK
Establishes the Western Australian Planning Commission (WAPC) and provides
Planning and Development Act 2005 for an efficient and effective land use planning system which promotes the
sustainable use and development of land in the State.
State Planning Strategy Provides a strategic framework and identifies principles, strategic goals and strategic
directions for planning and development in Western Australia.
State Planning Policies Provide the highest level of planning policy control and guidance in Western Australia.
Prepared under Part 3 of the Planning and Development Act 2005.
Provide for the comprehensive planning of regions, sub-regions or particular locations to guide
Regional & Sub-regional Strategies change and a basis for cooperative action to be taken by State and local government on land
use and development.
Operational Policies Guide decision making in relation to planning applications.
Position
A statement setting out the policy position of the WAPC with respect to a particular matter.
Statements
Guidelines Provide detailed guidance on the application of WAPC policies.
