# Legal And Licensing Notes

DraftCheck WA Core is not a lawyer, planner, building surveyor, certifier, or council decision-maker.

The system must not produce final compliance conclusions. Use wording such as:

- likely pass
- likely fail
- missing information
- needs human review
- not applicable
- unsupported by approved source library

Avoid final wording such as:

- compliant
- approved
- certified
- guaranteed approval
- submission-ready without human signoff

## Australian Standards

Do not scrape or store paid/proprietary Australian Standards full text. Store metadata only unless the user supplies lawfully licensed content and confirms its permitted use.

## Human Review

Exports include a liability notice and human signoff block. A generated response is a draft only and must be reviewed before submission.
